About Us


Hi there! My name is Sharon. As a former flying yoga instructor and actress, I have struggled plenty with body image and dieting. I am not the type of girl however, who can live off of just kale juice and half an apple. The truth is, I LOVE cookies! That is what led me to bake healthy desserts.

With years of research, I came to a conclusion that a whole food plant-based diet is the best possible diet. I do not follow the diet religiously, but that is what I mostly eat these days: legumes, vegetables, fruits, whole grains, nuts, and seeds - and that is what I make my desserts with! I now love eating healthy because I know that it makes me feel great on the inside and glow on the outside, regardless of my size or society’s idea of beauty.

Being also a former playwright and the daughter of a children’s book writer, I wanted to see if there was a way to encourage people to read books.
That’s what I envision Bookies Bakery to be: a place where people can read and play with words and art, while eating whole-plant based desserts! Sounds like a dream, right?
But like many dreams, mine will take some time. In the meantime, I will keep experimenting whole food plant-based recipes, deliver baked goods to lovely people, and keep cranking on my typewriter to hide quotes and stories in your cookies (along with a writing white cat).
Thank you for taking the time to read my story! I hope to hear all of your stories, while sharing a cookie together, very soon.
